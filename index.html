My Favorite Hobby
Catching small things

my hobby is catching insects. You might think bugs are gross, but belive me, they are cool. Think about it. bugs can do things we can't, like flying, stinging and climbing up things. Can you do that? You'd be crazy if you could. Ever since the day I learned about insects, I was hooked. They fascinated me. So to this day I continue to look deeply into nature and try to see something new every day.

my favorite bugs

My favorite insects are bees, butterflies, inchworms and caterpillars because theyare easy to find, but I also like catching dragonflies.

salamanders

salamanders are small creatures that resembe lizards. they can be found near water sources or in someone's backyard after it rains.

frogs

frogs are small hopping animals that are a little hard to catch and find. I have a special "frog pond" that i visit throughout the spring, summer and fall to find frogs.
